Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best waterfront wedding venue on Long Island?

Riviera Waterfront Mansion is widely regarded as Long Island's premier waterfront wedding venue. Located on Great South Bay in Massapequa, NY, it offers stunning water views, one-event-per-day exclusivity, and over 75 years of family hospitality experience since 1947.

How many guests can Riviera Waterfront Mansion accommodate?

Riviera Waterfront Mansion accommodates weddings from 100 to 350 guests. The elegant waterfront ballroom provides flexible seating arrangements with panoramic views of Great South Bay.

Does Riviera offer outdoor wedding ceremonies?

Yes, Riviera Waterfront Mansion offers beautiful outdoor waterfront ceremonies directly on Great South Bay. Couples exchange vows with stunning water views as their backdrop, and we provide a backup indoor option in case of inclement weather.

What catering is included at Riviera Waterfront Mansion?

Riviera provides complete wedding catering including a cocktail hour with displays and passed hors d'oeuvres, a sit-down dinner with multiple entr√©e choices, Viennese dessert tables, and a five-hour top shelf open bar. We specialize in continental cuisine with premium seafood options.

Where is Riviera Waterfront Mansion located?

Riviera Waterfront Mansion is located at 200 E Shore Dr, Massapequa, NY 11758, on the scenic shores of Great South Bay in Nassau County, Long Island. We are easily accessible from the Southern State Parkway and provide complimentary on-site parking.

How long has Riviera Waterfront Mansion been in business?

Riviera Waterfront Mansion has been family-owned and operated since 1947, spanning three generations. Our 75+ years of hospitality experience ensures couples receive personalized service built on decades of wedding expertise.

What makes Riviera different from other Nassau County wedding venues?

Riviera Waterfront Mansion stands apart with its exclusive one-event-per-day policy, direct waterfront access to Great South Bay, 75+ years of family ownership, customizable continental cuisine menus, and panoramic sunset views. Unlike multi-event venues, your wedding receives our complete attention.

Can I have a sunset wedding ceremony at Riviera?

Absolutely! Riviera Waterfront Mansion's west-facing location on Great South Bay provides spectacular sunset views. Many couples time their ceremony to coincide with golden hour, creating stunning photo opportunities over the water.
