Our Story

A Waterfront Mansion With a Legacy of Love

Riviera Waterfront Mansion began as a private estate along the Great South Bay in Massapequa, New York. Over time, it evolved into one of Long Island's most beloved wedding venuesa place where couples come for the water views, stay for the warmth of the staff, and leave with memories that feel like home.

Today, Riviera blends historic character with modern comfort. Hardwood floors, architectural details, and waterfront views create a backdrop that feels both timeless and completely your own.

One Wedding Per Day. Always.

Your wedding should never feel rushed or crowded.

At Riviera Waterfront Mansion, we host only one wedding per day, giving you:

  • Exclusive use of the mansion and waterfront grounds
  • A single, dedicated staff focused entirely on your event
  • A calm, comfortable timeline from prep to send-off
  • Privacy for you and your guests from start to finish

No other couples. No divided attention. Just your day, fully centered around your celebration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Planning Your Day at Riviera Waterfront Mansion

We want your planning process to feel clear and stress-free. Here are answers to the questions couples ask most often.

Do you host more than one event per day?

No. Riviera Waterfront Mansion hosts only one wedding per day. The entire mansion and waterfront grounds are dedicated to your event.

What is your guest capacity?

Our ballroom comfortably accommodates up to approximately 350 guests, depending on layout. We have a minimum of 100 guests.

Can we have our ceremony outdoors?

Yes. We offer outdoor waterfront ceremony options (weather permitting), as well as indoor ceremony locations if you prefer or need a weather backup.

Is there a bridal suite and groom's quarters?

Yes. We provide a private bridal suite and a separate space for the groom and wedding party to get ready, relax, and take photos.

Do you allow outside vendors?

We offer flexibility with vendors, including photographers, entertainment, and florists. We also maintain a preferred vendor list of professionals who know Riviera well. All outside vendors must be approved by the venue to ensure they meet our standards.

How does catering work?

Riviera provides in-house catering with experienced culinary teams offering menu options that fit a range of tastes and styles. Menu selections, tasting details, and specific packages are discussed as part of your proposal and planning process.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes. We can accommodate many dietary needs (such as vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and common allergies) when arranged in advance.

Is there onsite parking?

Yes. Riviera offers convenient parking for you and your guests, including complimentary valet service.

How far in advance should we book?

Popular datesespecially Saturdays and summer weekendsbook quickly. We recommend contacting us as early as possible to secure your preferred date.

How is pricing structured?

Our pricing is based on per-person rates, guest minimums for certain days of the week, and seasonal adjustments for busy months. Visit our Rates & Booking page or request a proposal for specific pricing for your date.

Where can I read reviews for Riviera?

You can find our reviews on multiple platforms including The Knot, WeddingWire, Google, Yelp, and Brides of Long Island. We've received over 500 five-star reviews from happy couples!

Does Riviera have social media I can follow?

Yes! Follow us @rivierawaterfrontmansion on Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Pinterest. We share real weddings, behind-the-scenes content, venue tours, and wedding inspiration.

How do I schedule a tour?

You can schedule a private tour by calling us at 516-541-5020 or by filling out our online contact form. Tours are available Tuesday through Sunday. We recommend booking in advance as our calendar fills quickly.
