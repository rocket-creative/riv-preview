Receptions at Riviera

Celebrate in an Elegant Waterfront Mansion

Riviera Waterfront Mansion's elegant ballroom accommodates up to 350 guests for wedding receptions on Long Island. As a one-event-per-day venue, your celebration receives our complete attention from ceremony through last dance. Our all-inclusive packages include gourmet continental cuisine, premium bar service, dedicated event coordinator, and 5+ hours of exclusive venue access. Located directly on Great South Bay in Massapequa, NY.

Questions Answered

Reception FAQs

How many guests can fit in Riviera's ballroom?

Riviera Waterfront Mansion's elegant ballroom comfortably accommodates up to 350 guests for wedding receptions. The flexible space includes room for dining, dancing, and entertainment while maintaining an intimate atmosphere.

Does Riviera host multiple weddings on the same day?

No. Riviera Waterfront Mansion is an exclusive one-event-per-day venue. Your wedding receives our complete attention from ceremony setup through the final dance. You'll never share the venue with another event.

What is included in a Riviera reception package?

Our all-inclusive reception packages include gourmet continental cuisine, premium open bar service, professional wait staff, linens and table settings, dedicated event coordinator, and 5+ hours of exclusive venue access. Couples may bring their own DJ, photographer, and florist.

Can I customize the menu and décor for my reception?

Absolutely. Our culinary team works with each couple to customize menus based on preferences and dietary requirements. The ballroom's elegant neutral décor serves as a beautiful canvas for any wedding style, from classic and traditional to modern and bold.
