Questions

Common Questions About Rates & Booking

Image Placeholder
Image Placeholder
Are these prices exact or estimated?
The calendar shows current starting per-person pricing for each date and day of the week. Your final proposal may differ slightly based on menu, bar selections, enhancements, and any updated pricing at the time you sign your agreement.
What does "++" mean?
"++" indicates that standard tax and service are additional. Your detailed proposal will show how tax and service are applied to your total.
Do you ever host more than one event per day?
No. Riviera Waterfront Mansion is a one-event-per-day venue. Once a date is reserved, that day is closed to any other event.
Do holiday weekends follow different pricing?
Holiday weekends and select peak dates may follow Saturday or premium pricing. Your proposal will clearly state the rate for your specific date.
Is there flexibility for non-peak dates or smaller events?
Yes. Non-busy months and weekday dates can sometimes offer more flexibility with guest counts and timing. If you're planning a smaller celebration, we encourage you to reach out so we can review options.
What if my guest count changes?
We understand that final guest counts can shift. Your contract will list the agreed minimum, and final billing is based on your guaranteed guest count provided closer to the event date.

Questions Answered

Pricing & Booking FAQs

How much does a wedding at Riviera Waterfront Mansion cost?

Wedding packages at Riviera start at $150 per person++ for 2026. Pricing varies by day of week and season. Thursday and Sunday weddings start at $150/person with 150-guest minimum. Saturday weddings start at $216/person with 250-guest minimum. Busy season months (May-November) have a 20% increase. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your date and guest count.

What is included in a Riviera wedding package?

Our all-inclusive packages include outdoor ceremony setup, cocktail hour with passed hors d'oeuvres, gourmet continental dinner service, premium open bar (5+ hours), professional wait staff, linens and table settings, dedicated event coordinator, and exclusive use of the venue for your celebration. You may bring your own DJ, photographer, and florist.

Does Riviera offer payment plans for weddings?

Yes, Riviera Waterfront Mansion offers flexible payment plans to help you budget for your celebration. Deposits secure your date, with remaining payments spread over scheduled milestones leading up to your wedding. Our team will work with you to create a payment timeline that fits your needs.

How far in advance should I book my wedding date at Riviera?

Popular Saturday dates in peak season (May-October) book 12-18 months in advance. For the most date flexibility, we recommend reaching out 12+ months before your preferred date. Thursday, Friday, and Sunday dates often have more availability with shorter lead times. Check our calendar or contact us to see current availability.

Are there discounts for off-peak wedding dates?

Yes! Our non-busy months (January, February, March, April, and December) offer lower per-person pricing without the 20% seasonal increase. Thursday and Sunday weddings also provide significant savings compared to Saturday events. Contact us to discuss budget-friendly date options that still deliver a beautiful Riviera celebration.
