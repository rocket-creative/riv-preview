2. Booking and Reservations

2.1 Reservation Requirements

To secure your wedding date at Riviera Waterfront Mansion, the following requirements apply:

  • A signed contract is required to confirm your booking
  • A $2,000 deposit is due at the time of signing
  • A $3,000 payment is due six months after signing
  • Final payment is due two weeks prior to your wedding date
  • All deposits may be paid by cash, credit card, or personal check
  • Final payment requires a certified or bank check only

2.2 Pricing

All pricing is subject to change without notice until a contract is signed. Pricing is based on guest count, date, and package selections. Credit card pricing differs and reflects transactional costs. All prices are in U.S. dollars.

2.3 Guest Count

Final guest count must be confirmed two weeks prior to your event date. The final guest count will determine the final pricing. We accommodate up to 350 guests.

3. Cancellation and Refund Policy

3.1 Cancellation by Client

If you cancel your event:

  • Deposits are non-refundable
  • Payments made more than 6 months in advance may be partially refundable at our discretion
  • No refunds will be provided for cancellations made less than 6 months before the event date
  • All cancellation requests must be made in writing

3.2 Cancellation by Riviera Waterfront Mansion

We reserve the right to cancel your event in the following circumstances:

  • Non-payment of required deposits or payments
  • Breach of contract terms
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, government restrictions, etc.)
  • In such cases, we will provide a full refund of all payments made

4. Payment Terms

Payment schedule:

  • Initial Deposit: $2,000 due at signing
  • Second Payment: $3,000 due six months after signing
  • Final Payment: Remaining balance due two weeks prior to event date

Late payments may result in additional fees. Failure to make payments as scheduled may result in cancellation of your event.

5. Venue Use and Restrictions

5.1 Exclusive Use

Riviera Waterfront Mansion hosts only one event at a time. You will have exclusive use of the entire venue, grounds, and facilities during your event.

5.2 Event Hours

Standard event hours are included in your package. Extended party time is available for an additional fee: $1,700 for ½ hour, $2,500 for 1 hour.

5.3 Vendor Requirements

You may bring your own DJ, photographer, videographer, and florist. All outside vendors must be licensed and insured. We provide in-house catering and bar service.

5.4 Prohibited Items and Activities

The following are prohibited:

  • Open flames (except approved candles)
  • Confetti, glitter, or similar materials
  • Smoking inside the venue
  • Illegal substances
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Any activity that may damage the venue or endanger guests

6. Catering and Bar Service

6.1 Catering

All catering is provided in-house by Riviera Waterfront Mansion. Outside catering is not permitted. Menu selections must be finalized two weeks prior to your event.

6.2 Bar Service

Bar service is provided in-house. Our base package includes 5 hours of top-shelf open bar. Additional bar hours are available for an additional fee. We comply with all local and state liquor laws.

6.3 Dietary Restrictions

We accommodate all dietary restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, kosher, gluten-free, and allergen-free options. Please inform us of all dietary requirements at least two weeks before your event.
