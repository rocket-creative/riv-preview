Questions Answered

Contact & Tour FAQs

How do I schedule a tour at Riviera Waterfront Mansion?

You can schedule a private tour by calling us at 516-541-5020 or submitting an inquiry through our contact form above. Our team will work with you to find a convenient time for your visit. Tours are available Tuesday through Sunday during office hours (11am-7pm).

What should I expect during a venue tour?

During your private tour, you'll walk through our entire venue including the ballroom, ceremony spaces, cocktail hour areas, and waterfront dock. Our team will discuss your vision, answer questions about packages and pricing, and check availability for your preferred dates. Tours typically last 45-60 minutes.

What is the best way to contact Riviera about my wedding?

For the quickest response, call us directly at 516-541-5020. You can also submit an inquiry through our contact form, and our team will respond within 24-48 hours. We're available Tuesday through Sunday, 11am to 7pm.

Where is Riviera Waterfront Mansion located?

Riviera Waterfront Mansion is located at 200 E Shore Dr, Massapequa, NY 11758, directly on the Great South Bay. We're easily accessible from both Nassau and Suffolk Counties, with convenient access from the Southern State Parkway and Sunrise Highway.
